$$F'^\lambda_{\mu\nu}(P) = \left(\frac{\partial x^\alpha}{\partial x'^\mu}\right)_P F^\rho_{\alpha\delta}(P) \frac{\partial x'^\lambda}{\partial x^\rho} \frac{\partial x^\delta}{\partial x'^\nu} - \frac{\partial^2 x^\rho}{\partial x'^\nu \partial x'^\mu} \frac{\partial x'^\lambda}{\partial x^\rho} = $$

$$F'^\lambda_{\mu\nu}(P) = F^\rho_{\alpha\delta}(P) \frac{\partial x^\alpha}{\partial x'^\mu} \frac{\partial x^\delta}{\partial x'^\nu} \frac{\partial x'^\lambda}{\partial x^\rho} + \frac{\partial^2 x^\rho}{\partial x'^\nu \partial x'^\mu} \frac{\partial x'^\lambda}{\partial x^\rho}$$
