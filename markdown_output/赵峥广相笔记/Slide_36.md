## 2. 挠率的几何意义

$$
\delta P = dx^M - \Gamma_{\beta}^{M} dx^{\alpha} \delta x^{\beta}
$$
$$
\delta P' = \delta x^M - \Gamma_{\beta}^{M} \delta x^{\beta} dx^{\alpha}
$$
$$
\begin{aligned}
\Delta &= \delta Q + \delta P - (\delta Q' + \delta P') \\
&= \delta x^M + \delta x^M - \Gamma_{\beta}^{M} \delta x^{\alpha} dx^{\beta} - \left[ \delta x^M + dx^M - \Gamma_{\beta}^{M} dx^{\beta} \delta x^{\alpha} \right] \\
&= \left[ \Gamma_{\alpha\beta}^{M} - \Gamma_{\beta\alpha}^{M} \right] \delta x^{\alpha} dx^{\beta} \\
&= \left[ \Gamma_{\nu\lambda}^{M} - \Gamma_{\lambda\nu}^{M} \right] \delta x^{\nu} dx^{\lambda} \\
&= 2\Gamma_{[\nu\lambda]}^{M} \delta x^{\nu} dx^{\lambda}
\end{aligned}
$$

实验证明我们的时空是无挠
