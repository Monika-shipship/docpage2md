# Slide 29

$$
A^\mu_{;\nu} B_\mu + A^\mu (B_{\mu,\nu} - \Gamma^\lambda_{\mu\nu} B_\lambda) = A^\mu_{,\nu} B_\mu + A^\mu B_{\mu,\nu}
$$

即

$$
A^\mu_{;\nu} B_\mu - A^\mu \Gamma^\lambda_{\mu\nu} B_\lambda = A^\mu_{,\nu} B_\mu
$$

$$
A^\mu_{;\nu} B_\mu - A^\lambda \Gamma^\mu_{\lambda\nu} B_\mu = A^\mu_{,\nu} B_\mu
$$

$$
\left(A^\mu_{;\nu} - \Gamma^\mu_{\lambda\nu} A^\lambda - A^\mu_{,\nu}\right) B_\mu = 0
$$

即 $A^\mu_{;\nu} = A^\mu_{,\nu} + \Gamma^\mu_{\lambda\nu} A^\lambda$

## 5. 高阶

$$
(T^\mu_\nu A_\mu B^\nu)_{;\lambda} = (T^\mu_\nu A_\mu B^\nu)_{,\lambda}
$$

$$
T^\mu_{\nu;\lambda} A_\mu B^\nu + T^\mu_\nu A_{\mu;\lambda} B^\nu + T^\mu_\nu A_\mu B^\nu_{;\lambda} = T^\mu_{\nu,\lambda} A_\mu B^\nu + T^\mu_\nu A_{\mu,\lambda} B^\nu + T^\mu_\nu A_\mu B^\nu_{,\lambda}
$$

$$
T^\mu_{\nu;\lambda} A_\mu B^\nu + T^\mu_\nu B^\nu \left(A_{\mu,\lambda} - \Gamma^\delta_{\mu\lambda} A_\delta\right) + T^\mu_\nu A_\mu \left(B^\nu_{,\lambda} + \Gamma^\nu_{\delta\lambda} B^\delta\right)
$$

$$
= T^\mu_{\nu,\lambda} A_\mu B^\nu + T^\mu_\nu A_{\mu,\lambda} B^\nu + T^\mu_\nu A_\mu B^\nu_{,\lambda}
$$
