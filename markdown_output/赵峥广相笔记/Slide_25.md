# Slide 25

前文对协变矢量有

1. $$A'_{\mu}(P \to \theta) = \left( \frac{\partial x^{\alpha}}{\partial x'^{\mu}} \right)_{\theta} A_{\alpha}(P \to \theta)$$

2. $$\delta A_{\mu}(P) = A_{\mu}(P \to \theta) - A_{\mu}(P) = \Gamma^{\lambda}_{\mu\nu}(P) \cdot A_{\lambda}(P) dx^{\nu}$$

可证明对逆变矢量有

1. $$A'^{\mu}(P \to \theta) = \left( \frac{\partial x'^{\mu}}{\partial x^{\alpha}} \right)_{\theta} A^{\alpha}(P \to \theta)$$

2. $$\delta A^{\mu}(P) = A^{\mu}(P \to \theta) - A^{\mu}(P) = -F^{\mu}_{\lambda\nu} A^{\lambda}(P) dx^{\nu}$$

(一会证)
