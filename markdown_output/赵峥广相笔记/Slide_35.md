# Slide 35

$$
A_{\lambda ;\mu ;\nu} - A_{\lambda ;\nu ;\mu} = A_\alpha \left[ \Gamma^\alpha_{\lambda\nu,\mu} - \Gamma^\alpha_{\lambda\mu,\nu} \right]
$$
对 $\alpha \to \rho$

$$
= A_\rho \left[ \Gamma^\rho_{\lambda\nu,\mu} - \Gamma^\rho_{\lambda\mu,\nu} \right]
$$

$$
= A_\rho \left[ \Gamma^\rho_{\lambda\nu,\mu} - \Gamma^\rho_{\lambda\mu,\nu} \right]
$$
（下划线标注部分）

$$
A_{\lambda ;\mu ;\nu} - A_{\lambda ;\nu ;\mu} = A_\rho R^\rho_{\lambda\mu\nu} - \cdots
$$
