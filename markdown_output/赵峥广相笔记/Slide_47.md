## 1. R之对称性

$R_{\sigma\lambda\mu\nu} = g_{\sigma\rho}R^{\rho}_{\lambda\mu\nu}$

(1) 后一对反对称  
(2) 前一对反对称 $R_{\sigma\lambda\mu\nu} = -R_{\lambda\sigma\mu\nu}$  
(3) 前一对指标与后一对是对称的 $R_{\sigma\lambda\mu\nu} = R_{\mu\nu\sigma\lambda}$  
(4) 里奇 $R^{\rho}_{\lambda\mu\nu} + R^{\rho}_{\nu\lambda\mu} + R^{\rho}_{\mu\nu\lambda} = 0$  
$\quad R_{\sigma\lambda\mu\nu} + R_{\sigma\nu\lambda\mu} + R_{\sigma\mu\nu\lambda} = 0$
