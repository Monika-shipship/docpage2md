## 碱金属原子的能级与光谱—碱金属原子光谱

第一辅线系(The first subordinate series)，又称漫线系(The diffuse series)，  
$nd \to 3p$ $\tilde{\nu}_n = \frac{R}{(3 - \Delta_p)^2} - \frac{R}{(n - \Delta_d)^2}$ $n = 3, 4, 5, \dots$  
$\Delta_d$是$l=2$的$d$能级的量子数亏损。

第二辅线系(The second subordinate series)，又称锐线系(The sharp series)，  
$ns \to 3p$ $\tilde{\nu}_n = \frac{R}{(3 - \Delta_p)^2} - \frac{R}{(n - \Delta_s)^2}$ $n = 4, 5, 6, \dots$

伯格曼线系(The Bergmann series)，又称基线系(The fundamental series)  
$nf \to 3d$ $\tilde{\nu}_n = \frac{R}{(3 - \Delta_d)^2} - \frac{R}{(n - \Delta_f)^2}$ $n = 4, 5, 6, \dots$  
$\Delta_f$是$l=3$的$f$能级的量子数亏损。
