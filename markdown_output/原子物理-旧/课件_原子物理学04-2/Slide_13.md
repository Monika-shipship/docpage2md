## 氢原子能级的超精细结构

超精细结构能级之间的跃迁频率为  
$ \nu \approx 1420 \ \text{MHz} $

对应的波长为  
$ \lambda \approx 21 \ \text{cm} $

The 21 cm line (1420.4 MHz) was first detected in 1951 by Ewen and Purcell at Harvard University
