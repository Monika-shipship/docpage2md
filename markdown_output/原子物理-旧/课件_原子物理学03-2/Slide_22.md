# 单电子(H)原子-角向方程

$N_{lm}$ 是归一化系数

$$
\int_{0}^{\pi} \int_{0}^{2\pi} Y_{lm}^{*}(\theta, \phi) Y_{lm}(\theta, \phi) \sin\theta \, d\theta \, d\phi = 1
$$

$$
N_{lm} = (-1)^m \left[ \frac{(2l+1)(l-m)!}{4\pi (l+m)!} \right]^{1/2}
$$

> **角向的球谐函数是 $L^2$ 和 $L_z$ 的本征函数：**

$$
\hat{L}^2 Y_{lm}(\theta, \phi) = l(l+1)\hbar^2 Y_{lm}(\theta, \phi)
$$

$$
\hat{L}_z Y_{lm}(\theta, \phi) = m\hbar Y_{lm}(\theta, \phi)
$$

$$
d\tau = dr \cdot r d\theta \cdot r \sin\theta d\phi = r^2 dr \sin\theta d\theta d\phi
$$
