# 单电子(H)原子-径向方程

## 方程的解
$$R(r) = R_{nl}(r) = N_{nl} e^{-\rho/2} \rho^l L_{n+l}^{2l+1}(\rho)$$
$$\rho = \left(-\frac{8m_e E}{\hbar^2}\right)^{1/2} r$$

## 连带拉盖尔多项式
$$L_{n+l}^{2l+1}(\rho) = \sum_{\nu=0}^{n-l-1} (-1)^{\nu+1} \frac{[(n+l)!]^2 \rho^\nu}{(n-l-1-\nu)! (2l+1+\nu)! \nu!}$$

## 归一化
$$\int_0^\infty |R_{nl}(r)|^2 r^2 dr = 1$$

## 归一化系数
$$N_{n,l} = -\left\{\left(\frac{2Z}{n a_0}\right)^3 \frac{(n-l-1)!}{2n[(n+l)!]^3}\right\}^{1/2}$$

$$d\tau = dr \cdot rd\theta \cdot r\sin\theta d\varphi$$
$$= r^2 dr \sin\theta d\theta d\varphi$$
