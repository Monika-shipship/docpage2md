## 泡利不相容原理和交换效应—两个电子的自旋波函数

$S = 0$ 单重态

$$\frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}[\sigma_+(1)\sigma_-(2) - \sigma_-(1)\sigma_+(2)]$$

$$
\begin{array}{c}
\text{（左侧图示）} \\
m_{s_1} = +\frac{1}{2} \\
\overrightarrow{s_1} \\
m_{s_2} = -\frac{1}{2} \\
\overrightarrow{s_2}
\end{array}
+
\begin{array}{c}
\text{（右侧图示）} \\
m_{s_2} = +\frac{1}{2} \\
\overrightarrow{s_2} \\
m_{s_1} = -\frac{1}{2} \\
\overrightarrow{s_1}
\end{array}
= 0
$$

两个电子的自旋取向看作是"反平行"的
