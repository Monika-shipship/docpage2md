# 泡利不相容原理和交换效应—交换效应

$$
\hat{H} = -\frac{\hbar^2}{2m_e}\nabla_1^2 - \frac{Ze^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r_1} - \frac{\hbar^2}{2m_e}\nabla_2^2 - \frac{Ze^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r_2} + \frac{e^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r_{12}} = \hat{H}_0 + \hat{H}'
$$

$$
\hat{H}_0 = -\frac{\hbar^2}{2m_e}\nabla_1^2 - \frac{Ze^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r_1} - \frac{\hbar^2}{2m_e}\nabla_2^2 - \frac{Ze^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r_2} = \hat{h}_1 + \hat{h}_2
$$

$$
\hat{H}' = \frac{e^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r_{12}}
$$

其中 $\hat{h}_i = -\frac{\hbar^2}{2m}\nabla_i^2 - \frac{Ze^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r_i} \quad i=1,2$

$$
\hat{H}' = \frac{e^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r_{12}}
$$

薛定谔方程

$$
\hat{H}_0 u^{(0)}(r_1, r_2) = E^{(0)} u^{(0)}(r_1, r_2)
$$
