## 自旋-轨道相互作用修正

$$ S \cdot L = \frac{1}{2}(J^2 - L^2 - S^2) $$
$$ = \frac{\hbar^2}{2}[j(j+1) - s(s+1) - l(l+1)] $$

$$ \Delta E_{ls} = U = \frac{1}{2} \frac{1}{m_e c^2} \frac{Z e^2}{4 \pi \varepsilon_0 r^3} S \cdot L $$

类氢离子处在 $u_{nlm}(r, \theta, \phi)$，得

$$ \left\langle \frac{1}{r^3} \right\rangle = \frac{Z^3}{a_0^3 n^3 l \left(l + \frac{1}{2}\right)(l+1)} $$

$$ \Delta E_{ls} = -E_n \frac{\alpha^2 Z^2}{n^2} \frac{n[j(j+1) - s(s+1) - l(l+1)]}{2l\left(l + \frac{1}{2}\right)(l+1)} \sim \alpha^2 E_n $$
