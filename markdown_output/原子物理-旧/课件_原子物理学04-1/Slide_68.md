Cross section of second apparatus: (a) tungsten oven of hydrogen dissociator, (b) movable slits, (c) electron bombarder cathode, (d) grid, (e) anode, (f) transmission line, (g) slots for passage of metastable atoms through interaction space, (h) plate attached to center conductor of r-f transmission line, (i) d.c. quenching electrode, (j) target for metastable atoms, (k) collector for electrons ejected from target, (l) pole face of magnet, (m) window for observation of tungsten oven temperature.

## Figure & Layout Description

Lamb的黑白半身肖像照片（1913-2008），照片下方有姓名和生卒年文字；下半部分是另一张黑白实验场景照片，显示研究人员在操作实验设备。页面底部有一条浅绿色背景的横条，内有黑色文字"Lamb and Retherford, Phys. Rev. 72, 241 (1947)"。整体页面背景为纯白色，文字主要使用黑色，除底部引用条外无其他颜色元素。装置图采用工程制图风格，包含剖面线、尺寸标注（1 inch）和箭头指示系统，各组件名称使用标准科学术语标注。
