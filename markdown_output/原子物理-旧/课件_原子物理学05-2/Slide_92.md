## 多电子原子光谱-电偶极跃迁的选择定则

### $LS$耦合：
$$
\begin{cases}
\Delta S = 0 \\
\Delta L = 0, \pm 1 \\
\Delta J = 0, \pm 1 \quad (J=0 \to J=0 \text{除外}) \\
\Delta M_J = 0, \pm 1 \quad (\text{当}\Delta J=0\text{时}, M_J=0 \to M_J=0\text{除外})
\end{cases}
$$

### $jj$耦合：
$$
\begin{cases}
\Delta j = 0, \pm 1 \quad (\text{跃迁电子}) \\
\Delta J = 0, \pm 1 \quad (J=0 \to J=0 \text{除外}) \\
\Delta M_J = 0, \pm 1 \quad (\text{当}\Delta J=0\text{时}, M_J=0 \to M_J=0\text{除外})
\end{cases}
$$
