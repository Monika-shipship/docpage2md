# Lamb位移

## 反常磁矩

1948年，库什(P. Kusch)和弗利(H.M. Foley)发现了电子的反常磁矩。

利用拉比(I.I Rabi)发展起来的原子束磁共振实验技术，精密测量了电子的自旋磁矩。

**Dirac理论**：$|\mu_{sz}| = |-g_s m_s \mu_B| = \mu_B$  
**Kusch实验**：$|\mu_{sz}| \approx 1.00119 \pm 0.00005 \mu_B$

In the experiment we measure these values for the electron, "dressed" with the interaction with its own field. It is impossible to measure "pure" mass or charge because we can not "switch off" electro-magnetic interaction!

*Polykarp Kusch (1911–1993)*  
*I. I. Rabi (1898–1988)*  
*H. A. Bethe (1906–2005)*

Kusch P., Foley H. M., Phys. Rev. 1948, 74: 256.
