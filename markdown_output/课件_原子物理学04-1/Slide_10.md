## 角动量量子数

$$ L^2 = L_x^2 + L_y^2 + L_z^2 $$
$$ = \frac{1}{2}(L_+ L_- + L_- L_+) + L_z^2 $$
$$ = L_- L_+ + L_z (L_z + \hbar). $$

**Z量子数最大为 $l$，这时**

$$ m \leq l $$

**当 $m = l$ 时**

$$ L_+ \psi = 0 $$
$$ L^2 \psi = \hbar^2 l(l+1) \psi $$
**所以**
$$ \lambda = l(l+1) $$
