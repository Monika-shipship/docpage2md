# 角动量相加

$$ L^2 = l(l+1)\hbar^2 $$
$$ L_z = m_l \hbar $$

$$ S^2 = s(s+1)\hbar^2 \quad s = \frac{1}{2} $$
$$ S_z = m_s \hbar \quad m_s = \pm \frac{1}{2} $$

$$ \mathbf{J} = \mathbf{L} + \mathbf{S} $$
$$ J^2 = j(j+1)\hbar^2 $$
$$ J_z = m_j \hbar $$

按照角动量相加法则，对于给定的量子数 $l$ 和 $s$

$$ j = l+s, \ l+s-1, \ \ldots, \ |l-s| $$

因为 $s = 1/2$，所以

$$ j = \left( l + \frac{1}{2} \right), \left| l - \frac{1}{2} \right| $$
