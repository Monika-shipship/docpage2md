## 泡利不相容原理和交换效应—交换效应

### 静电库仑斥力势能的修正
$$\hat{H}' = \frac{e^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 r_{12}}$$

#### (1) 基态 1s1s 或 $1s^2$
空间波函数 $u_S^{(0)}(\boldsymbol{r}_1, \boldsymbol{r}_2) = u_{100}(\boldsymbol{r}_1)u_{100}(\boldsymbol{r}_2)$

计算库仑斥力势能的平均值
$$\Delta E = \frac{e^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0} \left\langle \frac{1}{r_{12}} \right\rangle$$
得到
$$\Delta E = \frac{5}{8} \left( \frac{e^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0 a_0} \right) Z$$

对于氦原子 $Z=2$，$\Delta E \approx 34.0\,\text{eV}$

修正后的基态能量
$$E_{1s1s} = E_{1s1s}^{(0)} + \Delta E$$
$$= -108.8\,\text{eV} + 34.0\,\text{eV} = -74.8\,\text{eV}$$

#### (2) 电离能
$I.P. = -54.4\,\text{eV} - (-74.8\,\text{eV}) = 20.4\,\text{eV}$
