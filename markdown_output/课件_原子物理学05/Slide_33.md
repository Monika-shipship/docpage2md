# 泡利不相容原理和交换效应—交换效应

其中

$$
K = \frac{e^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0} \iint \frac{\left| u_{n_1 l_1 m_{l1}} (\boldsymbol{r}_1) \right|^2 \left| u_{n_2 l_2 m_{l2}} (\boldsymbol{r}_2) \right|^2}{r_{12}} d\boldsymbol{r}_1 d\boldsymbol{r}_2
$$

$$
J = \frac{e^2}{4\pi\varepsilon_0} \iint \frac{u^*_{n_1 l_1 m_{l1}} (\boldsymbol{r}_1) u_{n_2 l_2 m_{l2}} (\boldsymbol{r}_1) u^*_{n_2 l_2 m_{l2}} (\boldsymbol{r}_2) u_{n_1 l_1 m_{l1}} (\boldsymbol{r}_2)}{r_{12}} d\boldsymbol{r}_1 d\boldsymbol{r}_2
$$

| 修正后的激发态能量 | 相应波函数 |
|--------------------|------------|
| $E_{n_1 l_1, n_2 l_2} = E^{(0)} + \Delta E = E_{n_1} + E_{n_2} + K + J$ | $\psi(q_1, q_2) = u_S^{(0)}(\boldsymbol{r}_1, \boldsymbol{r}_2) \chi_A \quad S=0 \text{ 单重态}$ |
| $E_{n_1 l_1, n_2 l_2} = E^{(0)} + \Delta E = E_{n_1} + E_{n_2} + K - J$ | $\psi(q_1, q_2) = u_A^{(0)}(\boldsymbol{r}_1, \boldsymbol{r}_2) \chi_S \quad S=1 \text{ 三重态}$ |

两个电子自旋平行的三重态比自旋反平行的单重态能量低 $2J$
