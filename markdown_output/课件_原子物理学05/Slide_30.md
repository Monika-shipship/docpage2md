## 泡利不相容原理和交换效应—交换效应

### (2) 激发态能级，$1snl$

**空间波函数**

$$u_{S,A}^{(0)}(\mathbf{r}_1,\mathbf{r}_2) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{2}}[u_{100}(\mathbf{r}_1)u_{nlm_l}(\mathbf{r}_2) \pm u_{nlm_l}(\mathbf{r}_1)u_{100}(\mathbf{r}_2)]$$

**激发态能量**

$$E_{1snl}^{(0)} = E_1 + E_n = -2^2 \times 13.6eV - \frac{1}{2}m_ec^2\alpha^2\frac{2^2}{n^2}$$
$$= -54.4eV - \frac{1}{2}m_ec^2\alpha^2\frac{2^2}{n^2}$$

### (3) 电离能

$$n \rightarrow \infty \quad E_{1s\infty l}^{(0)} = -54.4eV$$

$$I.P. = -54.4eV - (-108.8eV) = 54.4eV$$

实验测得的氦的电离能是24.58 eV
