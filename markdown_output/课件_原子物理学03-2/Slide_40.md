# 跃迁和选择定则-电偶极跃迁

- 原子处在定态  
- 激发电偶极振荡  
- 辐射相同频率光子  

$$c_i^* c_f u_i^* u_f e^{i(E_i - E_f)t/\hbar} + c_i c_f^* u_i u_f^* e^{-i(E_i - E_f)t/\hbar}$$

$\psi_f \to \psi_i$  
$\psi_i \to \psi_f$  

于是  
$$\langle p \rangle = \langle -er \rangle = \int \psi_f^* (-er) \psi_i d\tau$$

跃迁速率  
$$\lambda_{fi} = \frac{\omega^3}{6\epsilon_0 h c^3} \left| \langle p \rangle \right|^2 = \frac{\omega^3}{6\epsilon_0 h c^3} \left| \int u_f^* (-er) u_i d\tau \right|^2$$
