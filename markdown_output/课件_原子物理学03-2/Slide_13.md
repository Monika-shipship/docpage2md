# 力学量的平均值、算符表示—平均值

## Cartesian coordinates
$(x, y, z)$

$$
x = r \sin\theta \cos\varphi \\
y = r \sin\theta \sin\varphi \\
z = r \cos\theta
$$

## Spherical coordinates
$(r, \theta, \varphi)$

$$
r = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2} \\
\theta = \arccos\left( \frac{z}{\sqrt{x^2 + y^2 + z^2}} \right) \\
\varphi = \arctan\left( \frac{y}{x} \right)
$$
