## (4) 其它常用的平均值

$$
\left\langle r^2 \right\rangle_{nlm} = \frac{n^4 a_0^2}{Z^2} \left\{ 1 + \frac{3}{2} \left[ 1 - \frac{l(l+1) - 1/3}{n^2} \right] \right\}
$$

$$
\left\langle \frac{1}{r} \right\rangle_{nlm} = \frac{Z}{a_0 n^2}
$$

$$
\left\langle \frac{1}{r^2} \right\rangle_{nlm} = \frac{Z^2}{a_0^2 n^3 (l + 1/2)}
$$

$$
\left\langle \frac{1}{r^3} \right\rangle_{nlm} = \frac{Z^3}{a_0^3 n^3 l(l + 1/2)(l + 1)}
$$
