## 单电子(H)原子-角向方程

$$
Y_{0,0} = \frac{1}{(4\pi)^{1/2}} \quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad\quad l = 0,1,2,3,\cdots
$$
$$
m = 0,\pm1,\pm2,\cdots\pm l
$$
$$
Y_{1,0} = \left(\frac{3}{4\pi}\right)^{1/2}\cos\theta \quad Y_{1,\pm1} = \mp\left(\frac{3}{8\pi}\right)^{1/2}\sin\theta e^{\pm i\phi}
$$
$$
Y_{2,0} = \left(\frac{5}{16\pi}\right)^{1/2}(3\cos^2\theta - 1) \quad Y_{2,\pm1} = \mp\left(\frac{15}{8\pi}\right)^{1/2}\sin\theta\cos\theta e^{\pm i\phi} \quad Y_{2,\pm2} = \left(\frac{15}{32\pi}\right)^{1/2}\sin^2\theta e^{\pm 2i\phi}
$$
$$
Y_{3,0} = \left(\frac{7}{16\pi}\right)^{1/2}(5\cos^3\theta - 3\cos\theta) \quad Y_{3,\pm1} = \mp\left(\frac{21}{64\pi}\right)^{1/2}\sin\theta(5\cos^2\theta - 1)e^{\pm i\phi}
$$
$$
Y_{3,\pm2} = \left(\frac{105}{32\pi}\right)^{1/2}\sin^2\theta\cos\theta e^{\pm 2i\phi} \quad Y_{3,\pm3} = \mp\left(\frac{35}{64\pi}\right)^{1/2}\sin^3\theta e^{\pm 3i\phi}
$$
$$
Y_{l,-m}(\theta,\phi) = (-1)^m Y_{lm}^*(\theta,\phi)
$$
$$
l = 0,1,2,3,4,5,\ldots \text{ 分别称为 } s,p,d,f,g,h,\ldots \text{ 态}
$$

## Figure & Layout Description
页面顶部有黑色粗体标题"单电子(H)原子-角向方程"，标题下方有一条蓝色水平分隔线（约2像素粗）。主体内容分为三部分：右侧竖排显示量子数取值范围 $l = 0,1,2,3,\cdots$ 和 $m = 0,\pm1,\pm2,\cdots\pm l$；中间区域以多行公式形式展示球谐函数 $Y_{l,m}$ 的具体表达式，公式按量子数 $l$ 分层排列（$l=0$ 单行，$l=1$ 两式并列，$l=2$ 三式并列，$l=3$ 四式分两行排列）；底部有量子数与原子态的对应说明。所有公式采用标准数学排版，黑色字体，希腊字母 $\theta,\phi$ 使用斜体，指数部分 $e^{\pm i\phi}$ 保持正体。页面背景为纯白色，无其他图形元素或颜色标注。
