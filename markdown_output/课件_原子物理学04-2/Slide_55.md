## 外磁场中的原子—强磁场

光谱线的频率变为  
$$
\nu = (E_2' - E_1') / h \\
= [E_2 - E_1 + 2\mu_B B(m_{s2} - m_{s1}) + \mu_B B(m_{l2} - m_{l1})] / h \\
= \nu_0 + \frac{2\mu_B B}{h}\Delta m_s + \frac{\mu_B B}{h}\Delta m_l \\
= \nu_0 + \frac{\mu_B B}{h}\Delta m_l \quad \text{选择定则 } \Delta m_s = 0
$$

用波数表示  
$$
\tilde{\nu} = \tilde{\nu}_0 + \frac{\mu_B B}{hc}\Delta m_l = 
\begin{cases} 
\tilde{\nu}_0 + \mathcal{L}, & \Delta m_l = +1 \\
\tilde{\nu}_0, & \Delta m_l = 0 \\
\tilde{\nu}_0 - \mathcal{L}, & \Delta m_l = -1 
\end{cases}
\quad \text{选择定则 } \Delta m_l = 0, \pm 1
$$
