## 碱金属原子的能级与光谱—量子数亏损

### 量子数亏损

$$E_{nl} = -\frac{1}{2} \mu \alpha^2 c^2 \frac{Z_{nl}^{*2}}{n^2} = -\frac{1}{2} \mu \alpha^2 c^2 \frac{1}{n^{*2}}$$

其中 $n^* = n / Z_{nl}^*$ 由于 $Z_{nl}^* > 1$，所以 $n^* < n$，

令 $n^* = n - \Delta_{nl}$ $\Delta_{nl}$ 称为**量子数亏损**

$$E_{nl} = -\frac{1}{2} \mu \alpha^2 c^2 \frac{1}{(n - \Delta_{nl})^2} = -\frac{Rhc}{(n - \Delta_{nl})^2}$$
