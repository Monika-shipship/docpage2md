## 能量守恒

$$ h\nu_0 + m_0c^2 = h\nu_S + \sqrt{p^2c^2 + m_0^2c^4} $$

## 动量守恒

$$ \frac{h}{\lambda_0} = \frac{h}{\lambda_S} \cos\varphi + mv \cos\theta $$

$$ \frac{h}{\lambda_S} \sin\varphi = mv \sin\theta $$

$$ \lambda_S - \lambda_0 = \frac{h}{m_0c}(1 - \cos\varphi) $$

$$ \lambda_C = \frac{h}{m_0c} \approx 0.00243\text{nm} \quad \text{电子的Compton波长} $$
